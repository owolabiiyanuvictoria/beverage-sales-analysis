## Project Overview
This project analyzes beverage sales data from January 2021 to December 2023 to evaluate revenue performance, sales volume, customer segmentation, and product trends. The analysis focuses on identifying key revenue drivers, customer behavior (B2B vs B2C), and performance across regions and product categories to support data-driven commercial decisions.

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $58.74M
- Total Sales (Units): 10.37M
- Average Unit Price: $5.84
- B2B Revenue: $44.87M
- B2C Revenue: $13.87M
- Total Orders: 420.94K
- Average Order Value (AOV): $139.54

## Business Questions
- How does revenue and sales volume trend over time?
- How do B2B and B2C customers differ in revenue contribution and order behavior?
- Which products drive the highest revenue and sales volume?
- How does performance vary by region and product category?
- Which customer segments contribute most to total quantity sold?

## Tools Used
- SQL (data extraction and aggregation)
- Power BI (data modeling, analysis, and dashboarding)

## Key Insights

### Sales & Revenue Performance
- Total revenue over the period reached $58.74M, driven primarily by B2B sales.
- B2B customers account for the majority of revenue, contributing over 75% of total sales value.
- Average unit price remains stable at $5.84, indicating consistent pricing across the period.

### Customer Segmentation
- B2B customers generate significantly higher revenue and quantity sold compared to B2C customers.
- B2C customers place more frequent orders but contribute a smaller share of total revenue.
- Quantity sold by B2B customers exceeds B2C by a wide margin, highlighting bulk purchasing behavior.

### Product Performance
- Alcoholic beverages are the top-performing category, generating the highest revenue across all categories.
- Juices, soft drinks, and water contribute smaller but consistent revenue shares.
- A limited number of products account for a significant portion of total sales value.

### Regional Performance
- Revenue varies across regions, with some regions consistently outperforming others in both B2B and B2C sales.
- B2B sales dominate revenue contribution across all regions.
- Regional performance shows stable demand rather than extreme volatility.

### Time-Based Trends
- Revenue and quantity sold show periodic fluctuations across months and quarters.
- Sales peaks align with specific periods, suggesting seasonal or demand-driven patterns.
- Long-term trends indicate steady performance rather than sharp declines or spikes.

## Outcome
This analysis provides clear visibility into customer behavior, product performance, and regional sales dynamics. The insights can support strategic decisions related to pricing, customer targeting, inventory planning, and category-level optimization.
